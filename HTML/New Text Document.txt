? - it specificy 0 or 1 occurance of any character

[a-Z] -both

[a-mA-M4-9] -
(?=.*[A-Z]) - at least 1 uppercase required
(?=.*[0-9]) - at least 1 uppercase required
(?=.*[!@]) - at least one special character

Quantifier
==========

{n} exactly n - numbers
{n,m} - minimum n and maximum m
{n, } - minimum n and maximum any

=======
